Agentic Diplomacy & B2B Negotiation

Phase 14 gives your platform a voice. The Diplomat Agent autonomously monitors all vendor relationships, detects risks, and negotiates better terms on your behalf — 24/7 without human involvement.

1. The Diplomat Agent

A specialized AI agent acts as your Chief Negotiation Officer, handling all vendor communications autonomously.

🔍 Vendor Monitoring

Continuously monitors health scores, uptime SLAs, and contract expiry dates across all vendors.

📨 Auto-Negotiation

Drafts and sends professional negotiation letters using platform usage volume as leverage.

💰 Savings Ledger

Every dollar saved via negotiation is logged in the negotiation_logs table for audit trails.

⚠️ Risk Detection

Automatic alerts for vendors with health score < 0.75 or contracts expiring within 30 days.
